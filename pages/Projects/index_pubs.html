The Death Dive:

The water-seeking behaviour induced by the endoparasitic worms to their normally hydrophobic arthropod hosts is one of the classic examples of host behaviour manipulation.

Genome assembly

Genome assembly and annotation of an organism is a critical first step to understand its biology. We are in process of genome assembly and annotation of the European earwig and its parasitic Mermithid nematode.

RNA-Seq

To study the genetic mechanisms of water-seeking behaviour we are using total RNA-seq analysis for both the host and the parasite at different stages of manipulation.

Bisulfite-Seq

To study the epigenetic mechanisms of water-seeking behaviour we are using whole genome bisulfite sequence analysis for both the host and the parasite at different stages of manipulation.
