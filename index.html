I hail from the lap of the Himalayas in Nepal, where the majestic mountains instilled in me a deep curiosity about the natural world. With dual PhDs from the University of Otago, New Zealand, and Northwest A&F University, China, I've dedicated my career to exploring the molecular mechanisms that drive biological complexity. Currently, I'm a Research Associate at the Harvard Chan Bioinformatics Core, where I combine my passion for genomics, transcriptomics, and epigenomics to unravel the mysteries of life at the molecular level.
